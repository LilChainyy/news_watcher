===============================================================================
                         KEY CONCEPTS
===============================================================================

Event Study:        Measuring abnormal returns around events
P-value:            Probability result is random (want < 0.05)
VaR:                Maximum likely loss at confidence level
CVaR:               Average loss in worst cases
Sharpe Ratio:       Return per unit of risk (want > 1)
Beta:               How much stock moves vs market
Correlation:        Statistical relationship between variables
Causation:          One thing causes another (harder to prove)
